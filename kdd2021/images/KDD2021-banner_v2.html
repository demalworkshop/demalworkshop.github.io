Jan. 10, 2021 >> Paper submission is open for both research track and applied data science track!


Jan. 10, 2021 >> To highlight, the Applied Data Science Track is distinct from the Research Track in that submissions should focus on applied work addressing real-world problems and systems demonstrating tangible impact/value in their respective domains.  It is required that a submission clearly specifies an audience or a group of users that have evidently benefited or will benefit from the solution presented in the submission.  Please note that papers that do not satisfy the requirements (e.g., a research track paper) might be rejected without a formal review.  Authors are strongly recommended to check out the CFPs carefully.
